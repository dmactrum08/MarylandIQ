# MarylandIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MarylandIQ collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the MarylandIQ website, ballot lookup tools, and related services.

MarylandIQ is a voter information platform focused on Maryland elections. The site aggregates public election and candidate information, provides ballot lookup and research tools, and may use automated systems to summarize or organize public information for voters.

This document is a working policy for the current MarylandIQ product scope and should be reviewed by counsel before public launch or submission to a platform that requires legal review.

## 1. Information We Collect

MarylandIQ may collect the following categories of information:

### Information you provide directly

- Address information you enter for ballot lookup
- Email address and message content if you submit a correction, feedback, or support request
- Any other information you choose to provide through forms or direct communication

### Information collected automatically

- IP address or approximate location derived from it
- Browser type, device type, operating system, and referring pages
- Pages viewed, links clicked, search actions, and other usage data
- Basic diagnostics and logs needed to operate, secure, and improve the service

### Public election and candidate information

MarylandIQ also stores and processes public information about candidates, contests, offices, ballot measures, and related election entities. This information may come from official government sources, campaign websites, public web pages, and other publicly available materials.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide ballot lookup and voter information tools
- Show candidate, race, office, and ballot measure pages
- Respond to corrections, questions, and support requests
- Improve site quality, accuracy, performance, and reliability
- Detect misuse, abuse, fraud, or security issues
- Maintain internal records about data pipeline runs and system performance

If MarylandIQ uses AI or other automated tools, those tools are used to summarize, classify, or organize public-source information. They are not intended to make eligibility, credit, employment, housing, or other legally significant decisions about users.

## 3. Ballot Lookup Data

When you use ballot lookup, you may enter an address or location-related information. MarylandIQ uses that information to determine the relevant precinct and ballot contests.

MarylandIQ aims to minimize retention of precise address data. Address information should only be retained as long as needed to return ballot results, operate the service, troubleshoot problems, prevent abuse, or maintain limited analytics and logs. MarylandIQ does not intend to build or sell address-based voter profiles.

## 4. Candidate and Public-Source Data

MarylandIQ processes public information about candidates and elections. This may include:

- Official filing information
- Office sought, party, jurisdiction, and election status
- Campaign website links and other public links
- Public statements or descriptions from official or public sources
- AI-generated summaries or tags derived from public-source material

MarylandIQ’s goal is to help voters understand public election information. If you believe a page contains inaccurate, outdated, misleading, or improperly sourced content, you may contact MarylandIQ to request review or correction.

## 5. Cookies and Analytics

MarylandIQ may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for:

- Basic site functionality
- Security and abuse prevention
- Analytics and performance measurement
- Remembering user preferences

If analytics tools are used, MarylandIQ will try to use privacy-conscious settings where practical. MarylandIQ may limit the collection of unnecessary personal data and may avoid cross-site advertising trackers.

## 6. How We Share Information

MarylandIQ does not sell personal information for money.

MarylandIQ may share information in the following limited circumstances:

- With service providers or infrastructure vendors that help operate the site
- With analytics, hosting, database, or security providers acting on MarylandIQ’s behalf
- If required by law, subpoena, court order, or legal process
- To investigate or address fraud, abuse, security incidents, or policy violations
- In connection with a business transfer, merger, acquisition, or asset sale

Public election and candidate information displayed on the site is, by design, intended for public viewing.

## 7. Data Retention

MarylandIQ retains information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including operating the service, maintaining election records, preserving public-interest content, complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing policies.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information. Public election records and related public-interest content may be retained for archival and historical purposes.

## 8. Data Security

MarylandIQ uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and MarylandIQ cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding personal information, including rights to access, correct, delete, or limit certain processing.

MarylandIQ is primarily an informational election platform, and much of the data it publishes is public-source information rather than user-submitted account data. Even so, you may contact MarylandIQ to:

- Request correction of inaccurate personal information you submitted directly
- Ask questions about how your information is handled
- Request review of allegedly inaccurate or misleading content

MarylandIQ will review requests in light of applicable law, public-interest considerations, technical feasibility, and the role of public election information in the service.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

MarylandIQ is not directed to children under 13, and it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If MarylandIQ learns it has collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate authorization, it will take reasonable steps to delete that information.

## 11. Third-Party Links and Services

MarylandIQ may link to third-party websites, including official government websites, campaign websites, news outlets, and other external resources. MarylandIQ is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

If MarylandIQ relies on third-party service providers for hosting, databases, analytics, or communications, those providers may process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

MarylandIQ may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, MarylandIQ may update the date at the top of this policy and take other appropriate steps to provide notice.
